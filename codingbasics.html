How robots use algorithms to work

Algorithms are a step of instructions for machines or computers to follow. They are very similar to a recipe for cooking, or instructions that might come with a model kit.

There are lots of awesome women in robotics and manufacturing who use algorithms to work!

  1. Dr. Ayanna Howard - Built robots for NASA to explore Mars! Now she makes robots that help kids learn.
  2. Dr. Cynthia Breazeal - Created the first friendly robot (Jibo) that talks like a human!
  3. Ruzena Bajcsy - A "robot grandma" who teaches robots to sense the world (like feeling how hard to hug someone).
  4. Debbie Sterling (Manufacturing) - Founded GoldieBlox, a company that makes engineering toys for girls!
  5. Dr. Fei-Fei Li - Teaches robots to "see" using cameras (like recognizing a kitten vs. a puppy).
